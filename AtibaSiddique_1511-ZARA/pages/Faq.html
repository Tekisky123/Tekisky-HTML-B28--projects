FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



1. What types of jewelry do you offer?
We offer a wide range of jewelry including necklaces, bracelets, rings, and earrings made from high-quality materials such as gold, silver, and precious gemstones.

2. Do you offer custom jewelry designs?
Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry designs. You can work with our designers to create a unique piece that reflects your personal style and preferences.

3. What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

4. How do I care for my jewelry?
To keep your jewelry looking its best, we recommend cleaning it regularly with a soft cloth and storing it in a jewelry box when not in use. Avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals and extreme temperatures.

5. Do you offer gift wrapping services?
Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping services for all purchases. You can select this option at checkout.
