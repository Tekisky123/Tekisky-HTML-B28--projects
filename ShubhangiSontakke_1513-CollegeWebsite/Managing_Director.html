Managing Director Message

We, Matoshri Pratishthan's Group Of Institutions at Kupsarwadi in Nanded, Maharashtra,are focused on acquiring knowledge rather than imparting knowledge wherein teachers act as facilitators. The learning process is made more enjoyable by adopting an unconventional methodology of education where students are encouraged to learn through research & model-making, leading to greater retention. Our aim is to ensure all-round development of the students personality..
